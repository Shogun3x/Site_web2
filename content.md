# Content Guidelines

## Language
Primary language: French (Canada)
Tone: clear, professional, accessible

## Messaging Principles
- Focus on problems and outcomes
- Avoid buzzwords without context
- Emphasize reliability and ROI

## Core Sections
- Hero (value proposition + CTA)
- Problems (pain points)
- Services
- Use cases / examples
- Methodology
- Final CTA

## Call to Action
Primary:
- Réserver un appel

Secondary:
- Voir des cas d’usage
- Contact par courriel

## Proof & Credibility
- Clear process
- Concrete examples
- Local presence
